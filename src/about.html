About

For years, thousands of home shoppers have turned to Beta house to find their dream home. Operated by Move, Inc., Beta house offers a comprehensive list of for-sale properties, as well as the information and tools to make informed real estate decisions. Today, more than ever, Beta house is The Home of Home Search. Beta house also offers homeowners a bevy of useful tools and resources through its website. This website allows property owners to manage their home like the important investment it is by tracking their home’s value over time, researching and managing home improvements, and scouting other similar properties in the neighborhood.
